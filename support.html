FAQ

What is active EQ?

Active EQ sound equalization technology that tunes the frequency response to closely match Bose headphone audio. Active EQ is a result of decades of research studying how sound travels to the ear to create a more lifelike audio performance.

How comfortable are Bose Sport Earbuds?

The earbuds are designed to be comfortable enough to be worn for long periods of time. They stay firmly in place no matter how vigorously you move. The eartips are flexible and spread pressure evenly across your ear.

Are Bose Sport Earbuds water and sweat resistant?

Yes they are. The Bose Sport Earbuds rated IPX4, that means they are protected against sweat and splashing water from any angle. The interior of the charging case is also resistant to water or sweat.

Can you take calls with the Bose Sport Earbuds?

Yes you can. The Bose Sport Earbuds have an integrated dual-microphone array in the right earbud to use them during calls. You can also use them with other voice over apps such as facetime and zoom.
